What is Join?

Join is a Kanban-based project management tool designed to help teams organize their tasks efficiently.

How to use the Board

The board is divided into four columns: To do, In progress, Awaiting Feedback, and Done. You can drag and drop tasks between these columns to update their status.

Creating Tasks

Click on "Add Task" in the sidebar or the plus button in the board columns to create a new task. Fill in the title, description, due date, and assign it to your team members.
